Segment Definitions

Volume-First Approach

Contacts are classified into 4 segments without scoring. The goal is to build a high-volume pipeline of relevant LP prospects across the target geographies.

Geo focus: Singapore, Hong Kong, UAE, Saudi Arabia

4 LP Target Segments

FO
Family Offices
Single & Multi-Family Offices, Private Investment Offices. Both crypto-native and traditional. Largest segment.
HNWI
High Net Worth Individuals
Individual investors, exited founders, angel investors, private investors, VHNWIs. Contact directly.
FoF
Crypto Funds of Funds
Fund of Funds, Digital Asset FoFs, Crypto Asset Managers, Emerging Manager Platforms. Direct allocators.
Other
Others
Wealth Managers, RIAs, Private Bankers, Peer Crypto Funds, DAO Treasuries, Crypto VCs. Relevant but secondary.

Geographic Focus

Asia: Singapore, Hong Kong
Middle East: UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi), Saudi Arabia (Riyadh, Jeddah)

Existing contacts outside these geos are kept. New searches focus on these markets.
